Numerical methods (FE, basis type,incompressibility type) : Cubic Lagrange Hexahedral Finite elements for deformation, Linear elements for pressure, Lagrange Multiplier/

The degrees of freedom (DOF) include the displacement field and the hydrostatic pressure field, and include fixed/dirichlet DOFs. 
Meshes in order are
4x1x1
8x2x2
16x4x4
50x5x5
